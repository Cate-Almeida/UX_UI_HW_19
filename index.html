My UX Skills

UX/UI Design

I craft unique user experiences based upon the principals of design. I also ensure the user interface is properly intergated with the design elements to create a seamless experience for the users!

User Research

User research is my bread and butter! I use in-person and online surveys to gather responses for my projects to ensure that I meet the needs of users and stakeholders alike.

Prototyping

From wireframe to prototype, designing page layouts are my specialty. I enjoy the challenges of design research and putting various elements on a page together to create a work of functioning art.
